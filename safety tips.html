Travel Safety Tips

Stay safe while exploring India with important travel precautions, emergency contacts, and smart travel advice.

Important Safety Tips

📍 Keep Documents Safe

Carry copies of passports, IDs, and travel tickets.

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink clean bottled water and stay hydrated during trips.

🚖 Use Trusted Transport

Prefer official taxis and verified travel services.

📱 Emergency Contacts

Save emergency numbers and hotel contact information.

🌙 Avoid Unsafe Areas

Avoid isolated places late at night during travel.

🎒 Keep Belongings Safe

Keep wallets, phones, and bags secure in crowded places.

Travel Checklist

✅ Carry identity proof
✅ Keep power bank and charger
✅ Book hotels in advance
✅ Keep emergency cash
✅ Share live location with family
✅ Keep medicines and essentials

Emergency Assistance

India provides emergency helplines, police support, hospitals, and tourist assistance centers across major cities.

Emergency Number: 112
