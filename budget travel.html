Budget Travel Guide

Explore India without spending too much. Discover affordable transport, cheap hotels, street food, and smart money-saving travel tips.

Money Saving Tips

🚆 Travel By Train

Trains are affordable and connect almost every city in India.

🍜 Eat Local Food

Street food and local restaurants save money and taste amazing.

🏨 Stay In Hostels

Budget hostels are cheaper and perfect for backpackers.

🎒 Carry Essentials

Carry reusable bottles and snacks to avoid extra expenses.

📅 Travel Off-Season

Hotel and flight prices become cheaper during off-season.

🚌 Use Public Transport

Buses and metros help reduce daily travel costs.
